What is Centralia Car OS?

Centralia Car OS is a futuristic example for user interactions both within the car and outside of the car.

It utilizes the car’s windshield, a console screen, and personal smartphones to create a seamless, multimodal driving experience. Through thoughtful designs and trackings across physical devices, Centralia would like to use ride-related personal data to provide a better user experience for both drivers and passengers.

Design Language

Through team brainstorm, we identify the following common themes across driving experience, where we identify our design opportunity and design language.

“Essential” is our design language. Being minimal and sleek, powerful yet not distracting, “Essential” creates a unique experience for drivers that incorporates intuitive & responsive designs to promote safety. It becomes one with the road and one with you.
